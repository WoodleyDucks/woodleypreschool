Welcome to Woodley Pre-School

We are a friendly community group in Romsey, Hampshire who offer Early Years care and education for children aged from 2 years 9 months to school age. We are lucky enough to be able to meet in a new, bright and modern purpose built playroom next to Cupernham Infant School, with our own entrance and secure outside play area.

We organise our sessions so that the children can choose from a range of activities and experiences both inside and outside our playroom and also in the extensive Cupernham school grounds. Our supportive staff are on hand to help children gain new skills, make friendships and have fun.

If you would like to meet us please telephone the Pre-school where we will be happy to arrange your visit.
